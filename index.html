Connect 4

Connect 4 is a two-player connection strategy game played on a vertical 7-by-6 grid. Players take turns dropping discs into the columns; the first to align four of their discs in a row, column, or diagonal wins. The default challenge starts the solver-backed Very Hard computer first, making the AI virtually unbeatable when it moves first. The app is free and runs in your browser with no signup, ads, or download.
