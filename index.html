Connect 4

Connect 4 is a two-player connection strategy game played on a vertical 7-by-6 grid. Players take turns dropping discs into the columns; the first to align four of their discs in a row, column, or diagonal wins. Play locally on one device with a friend, or challenge an AI opponent at four difficulty levels. The app is free, works offline as an installable PWA, and is available in English, Hebrew, and Thai.
